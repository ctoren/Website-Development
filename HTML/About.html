Welcome to the About page!

Finding jobs in the entertainment industry can be quite a challenge! The industry is extremely competitive, and everyone is looking to find their next big break. It is true that having connections in the industry is the easiest way to get a job, even if you might not be as qualified for it. However, no need to worry! You still have the chance to find the job of your dreams! This site was started in order to help industry hopefuls (like you!) have an equal chance at getting your foot in the door! The administrators of this site will post jobs as often as possible, but we also need your help! If you or someone you know has a great job opportunity to post, please share it with us and our community. Together we will help each other land amazing jobs in this glamorous industry!
